# Resources

Check out these great resources for information about the fundamentals of building science and energy modeling.

 - Building Science: 
     - [Building Science Education Series (solardecathlon.gov)](https://www.solardecathlon.gov/building-science.html)
 - Energy Modeling: 
     - [The Energy Modeling Knowledgebase](http://www.bemcyclopedia.com/)
     - [Building Energy Modeling 101 (Whole Building Design Guide)](https://www.wbdg.org/continuing-education/dod-courses/estcp/estcp3-1)
	 - [The International Building Performance Simulation Association (IBPSA)](https://www.ibpsa.us/)
	 
## Forum

[Unmet Hours](https://unmethours.com/questions/) is an externally hosted forum that serves as a question-and-answer website for the building energy modeling community.

When posting a new question, consider adding the "sketchbox" tag to your post.
